Trent University - MyCommunity Website

During my time at Trent University I helped lead the re-design of the MyCommunity website. This website was used by alumni and donors to keep up with Trent University news stories, access alumni services and access donation forms. This website was built on Blackbaud's NetCommunity platform allowing connections to their Raiser's Edge Software which is a database software used for fundraising and non-profits.
